How to Apply for a New Driving License

If you're applying for your first driving license, follow these steps to complete your application successfully.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important

Applicants must be at least 16 years old to apply for a learner's permit and 18 years old for a full driving license.

Application Steps:

  1. Complete Online Registration
    Register on the online portal with your IC number and personal details.
  2. Upload Required Documents
    Scan and upload your IC, passport photo, and proof of residence.
  3. Medical Examination
    Visit an authorized clinic for a medical fitness examination.
  4. Theory Test
    Study the Highway Code and pass the written theory examination.
  5. Practical Training
    Complete minimum 20 hours of practical driving lessons with a licensed instructor.
  6. Practical Test
    Schedule and pass the practical driving test at your chosen test center.
  7. License Issuance
    Upon passing, your license will be issued within 5-7 working days.
üí° Tip

Book your theory and practical tests early as slots fill up quickly, especially during peak seasons.

Processing Time:

  • Online application review: 3-5 working days
  • Theory test scheduling: Available within 2 weeks
  • Practical test scheduling: 4-6 weeks after theory test
  • License issuance: 5-7 working days after passing

How to Renew Your Driving License

Renewing your driving license is a simple process. You can start the renewal process up to 90 days before your license expires.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important

Driving with an expired license is an offense and may result in fines. Renew your license before it expires to avoid penalties.

Renewal Steps:

  1. Login to Portal
    Access the online portal using your citizen login credentials.
  2. Select Renewal Option
    Navigate to "License Renewal" and enter your existing license number.
  3. Update Information
    Verify and update your personal details if necessary.
  4. Upload New Photo
    Upload a recent passport-sized photograph (if required).
  5. Medical Certificate
    For ages 65+, upload a recent medical fitness certificate.
  6. Pay Renewal Fee
    Complete payment online using credit/debit card or online banking.
  7. Collect New License
    Receive your renewed license by mail or collect from the office.

Validity Periods:

  • Ages 18-64: 10 years validity
  • Ages 65-74: 5 years validity
  • Ages 75+: 3 years validity (annual medical required)
üí° Tip

Set a reminder 90 days before your expiry date to start the renewal process early.

03 Required Documents
‚ñº

Documents Required for Application/Renewal

For New Applications:

  • Original IC/Passport (for verification)
  • Certified copy of IC/Passport (color)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (white background, no glasses)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or bank statement, not older than 3 months)
  • Medical fitness certificate from authorized clinic
  • Birth certificate (for first-time applicants under 21)
  • Theory test pass certificate
  • Practical training completion certificate

For Renewals:

  • Original existing driving license
  • Updated IC/Passport (if details changed)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Medical certificate (for ages 65+ only)
  • Proof of address (if address changed)
üì∏ Photo Requirements
  • White or light-colored background
  • Taken within the last 6 months
  • Full face visible, no head covering (except religious purposes)
  • No glasses, sunglasses, or tinted lenses
  • Natural expression, mouth closed
  • Size: 35mm x 45mm

Digital Document Specifications:

  • File format: JPG, PNG, or PDF
  • Maximum file size: 5MB per document
  • Minimum resolution: 300 DPI
  • Documents must be clear and legible
  • All text must be readable

Common Questions

Q: How long does it take to get a driving license?

For first-time applicants, the entire process typically takes 2-3 months, including theory and practical tests. If you're just renewing, it takes 5-7 working days.

Q: Can I drive while my renewal is being processed?

If you submitted your renewal before expiry, you can continue driving for up to 30 days after expiry while waiting for your new license.

Q: What happens if I fail the practical test?

You can retake the test after 7 days. You'll need to pay the test fee again and schedule a new appointment.

Q: Can I apply for an international driving permit?

Yes, once you have a valid local driving license. The international permit is valid for 1 year and costs $25.

Q: What if I lost my driving license?

Report it immediately through the online portal or at any service center. You can apply for a replacement license for $30.

Q: Do I need to retake tests when renewing?

No, renewal does not require retaking any tests unless your license has been expired for more than 3 years.

Q: Can someone else collect my license for me?

Yes, with a signed authorization letter and copies of both ICs (yours and the representative's).
